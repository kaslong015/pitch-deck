Scientific Foundation

LCT Pegmatite Mineral Systems Model

Components of Our System

Source

Younger granite province provides fertile parental magmas with high fractionation levels and lithium-enriched melts.

Pathway

Second-order shear zones and brittle-ductile structures focus late magmatic fluids into favorable host rocks.

Trap

Pegmatite swarms within cupola positions and structural dilations concentrate lithium minerals.

Evidence

Coherent Li, Cs, Ta anomalies with K/Rb ratios and Nb/Ta systematics confirming highly fractionated LCT pegmatites.

Global Analogues Validate Our Model

  • Greenbushes (Australia): World's largest hard-rock lithium operation with similar structural controls
  • Bikita (Zimbabwe): 60+ years of production from African LCT pegmatites
  • Tanco (Canada): Zoned pegmatite with lithium-tantalum mineralization
  • Kings Mountain (USA): Shear-controlled spodumene swarms

Critical Uncertainties

What We're Working to Resolve

Transparent assessment of geological and technical questions—and our systematic approach to answering them:

1. Granite Fertility & Fractionation

Question: Which younger granite intrusions are truly lithium-fertile?

Approach: Detailed geochemical and isotopic characterization to establish fertility indicators and predict additional pegmatite clusters.

2. Fluid Transport Pathways

Question: How effectively do structural features control pegmatite emplacement?

Approach: Drone magnetometry, field mapping, and structural modeling to quantify pathway controls on lithium-rich melt focusing.

3. Grade Continuity & Zonation

Question: What are the vertical and lateral grade variations within pegmatite swarms?

Approach: Systematic core drilling, trench sampling, and assay programs to define grade consistency and economic potential.

4. Hydrothermal Overprinting

Question: Have secondary processes redistributed lithium or introduced other mineral phases?

Approach: Mineralogical studies and alteration mapping to distinguish high-grade primary pegmatites from metamorphosed equivalents.

5. Model Calibration & Scaling

Question: How do we optimize predictive models from regional to deposit scale?

Approach: Continuous data integration from drilling and core logging to refine machine learning frameworks and reduce targeting uncertainty.

"Each uncertainty represents not a limitation, but an opportunity to deepen our understanding of Nigeria's lithium-bearing systems and establish a robust, repeatable exploration model."
